DO NOT store:

- Opinions, feelings, greetings, threats, banter, or how someone reacts.
- Anything about the player, or about the current conversation itself.

- "concerns": the SPECIFIC named people or a specific business/household the fact is ABOUT — who owes what, who claims what, whose property it is, who did what to whom. List their names.
